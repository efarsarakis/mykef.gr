Services

We undertake:

  • Certificates and Civil Registry Records (such as certificates of family status, marriage, civil partnership, birth, death, large family status, criminal record applications, court-issued solvency status, etc.)
  • Solemn declarations, authorizations, and certification of document signatures
  • Enrollment applications for all levels of education (from nursery and kindergarten to upper secondary school, including EPAS vocational schools, EPAL technical schools, SAEK continuing education structures, etc.)
  • Public Employment Service (DYPA – formerly OAED): Registration in the unemployment registry, applications for benefits (for seasonal workers, maternity, etc.), and issuance of unemployment certificates.
  • ASEP (Supreme Council for Civil Personnel Selection): Creation of applicant registry, submission of applications and objections, municipal job announcements, etc
  • For third-country nationals: Submission and renewal of residence permits, issuance of special permanent residence permits, and document authentication via the Hague Convention Apostille.
  • EFKA – IKA – Social Insurance Institutions Services and procedures provided include:Applications for insurance eligibility, sickness benefits, submission of supporting documents for funeral expense reimbursement, employment history certificates, issuance of the European Health Insurance Card (EHIC), registration certificates, transfer of survivor’s pensions, and reimbursement claims for private nursing care expenses.
  • Health:This encompasses the activation of the electronic prescribing system, the provision of diagnostic and laboratory examination results, the issuance of certified copies of individual health records, the electronic child health booklet, as well as the facilitation of electronic appointment scheduling at Primary Healthcare Units (TOPY), Health Centers, and Public Hospitals, in accordance with applicable health regulations and administrative procedures.
  • Benefits and compensations: Minimum Guaranteed Income (KEA), child benefits (A21), optical allowances, heating allowance, housing allowance, student allowance, foster care benefits, birth allowance, book and entertainment vouchers, social tourism programs for pensioners, and public welfare employment programs.
  • Accounting Services: Issuance of Tax Identification Number (TIN) and password (for individuals, minors, and foreign residents), registry updates, vehicle circulation tax payments, declarations of non-seizable bank accounts, and registrations in the National Contact Registry.
